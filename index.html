AquaSentry: Smart Water Sentinel

Integrated Solar-Powered Water Quality Monitoring with MicroSD Logging and Manual Trigger Button.

1. Sensor Array & Local Processing

Sensor Array (TDS, pH, Turbidity, Temp)

Core sensors measure water quality at scheduled intervals or manually via button trigger.

Display activates only during measurement to save battery.

ESP32 Control Unit

Manages sensor readings, applies calibration & temperature compensation, and logs data to MicroSD.

Supports deep sleep and manual button wake-up to conserve energy.

Solar & Battery System

5V, 2W solar panel charges 3.7V 2500mAh Li-ion battery via TP4056 & LM2596. Ensures autonomous operation.

Deep sleep maximizes battery endurance.

MicroSD & Display Unit

Display shows real-time readings; MicroSD logs all historical data.

Local storage ensures non-volatile backup without any network.
